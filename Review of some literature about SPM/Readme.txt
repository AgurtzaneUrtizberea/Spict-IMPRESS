The document "SPModels_Review.pdf" is a brief review about the main ideas of some paper of Surplus Production models, as you can see, this review includes 
papers related to ASPIC (Prager, 1994 and Prager et al., 1996), to SPiCT (Pedersen, 2017) and to JABBA (Winker et al., 2018).

Please, note that, the review is a working document, hence is open to improvements in the writting, correction of typos or mistakes, and addition of other
interesting papers that we can review.

The document can be updated due to the previous comments, you can check the status of the document following our repository and its commits.
